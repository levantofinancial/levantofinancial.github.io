Service Level Agreement & Payment

This document describes the engagement agreement and payment plan you will enter into to use Levanto Financial Inc.’s products and services.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Levanto Financial will help you manage your Cash Flow on a daily basis. The Cash Flow Plan you and your Household CFO created during your Cash Flow Planning & Analysis session will be the baseline plan Levanto will help you implement and manage. Levanto will only have view access to your financial accounts. Through our secure onboarding portal, you will grant us view access to the financial accounts you would like us to monitor.

You will designate one of your bank accounts as your Required Expense account. Your Required Expense Account is your primary checking account with your bank. This account will remain under your ownership and control. Levanto will monitor the account you designate as a Required Expense Account. We monitor this account to ensure you have the funds necessary to issue payments to your creditors, to cover your periodic bills,to cover automatic debits and to proactively set funds aside for expenses you will incur periodically in the future.

The funds that are set-aside for your future expenses will be called Virtual Savings. Your Virtual Savings will exist in your account so long as you have sufficient funds in your Required Expense account to cover all of your payments and your projected incomes are deposited as planned.

Levanto’s Household CFOs and Client Service Representatives will be available throughout the business day to help you with your financial needs. Our commitment to you is be responsive, proactive and to ensure your personal financial goals are being achieved. We will periodically reach out to you for account reviews or to discuss opportunities we see while monitoring your financial account. If we see that the direction of your Cash Flow Plan is not helping you achieve your financial objectives set forth during your Cash Flow Planning & Analysis session we will proactively let you know so that we can work with you to get back on track.

Automatic Withdrawals

Levanto does not have the authority, permission, or capability to initiate or stop any transactions from your bank or credit card accounts. Levanto has read-only access, that is provided and secured via bank-grade encryption protocols.

Third Party Providers

Levanto Financial will assist you with setting up the third party providers, such as a data aggregation service firm, that are necessary to manage and monitor your account. These providers will allow Levanto to view your Levanto Required Expense Account, view your credit card accounts and any other accounts where you would like us to monitor your personal financial transactions.

TERMS OF SERVICE:

Levanto Financial, Inc. (“Levanto”) will act on your instructions, subject to the terms in this agreement. If you wish to make any alterations to your Cash Flow Plan, you must notify Levanto. Your Cash Flow Plan changes continually because, for example, utility bills vary each month, debt and loans get paid off and bills are retired. Levanto shall constantly review your Cash Flow to ensure the integrity of the plan remains intact.

Errors/Disputes and Resolutions

You must notify Levanto promptly if there is an error in your Cash Flow Plan. You must notify Levanto in writing, by email or by contacting our offices. It is essential that you give Levanto as much information as you have to help Levanto to resolve your concern and provide Levanto with sufficient time to remedy your concern. You authorize Levanto to monitor and record your calls to for training and verification purposes.

Termination

You can terminate this agreement for any reason by giving Levanto 30 days’ notice in writing provided that all of your administration fees are up to date. If you request termination of this agreement prior to your fees being paid in full, the account administration fee will remain payable by you until such time that all fees ares fully repaid. Levanto can terminate this agreement for any reason by giving you notice. Any outstanding balance will become immediately due and payable by you. Levanto does not and cannot make bill payments on your behalf.

If you currently have direct debit payments being taken from any bank account please read this carefully:

Levanto is not authorized to change details that you have previously provided to a creditor who direct debits your bank account. If you wish to cancel a direct debit, please ensure that you contact any creditor that direct debits your bank account and check to see whether they accept payment by another method other than by direct debit.
